# Project 01- Cruisin Texas Webpage

## 1. What Is This?
This repository will serve as a collection for my project which will be a website of listed upcoming Hot Rod Car Shows for the state of Texas and where visitors/attendees can gather more information regarding the venue and the event. The website will act as a resource to find other associated events and to gain more insight to the surrounding areas, accommodations, and amenities.


## 2. Why Does It Exist?
I built this website to give Hot Rod enthusiasts and owners some insight for certain shows they may have considered attending for the first time and unsure of what to expect. My website will give them a better picture of what is offered at these shows so they may be able to plan accordingly when they travel to attend. First time goers who may be traveling will appreciate this, as they can also read reviews from past attendees. The purpose is to provide as much inoframtion as possible to paint a clearer picture of what they can expect when attending the shows, along with what other events may be associated for when after the car show has finished for the day.The site also helps attendees plan beyond the show itself, whether that means booking an overnight stay or exploring local attractions the host city has to offer.


## 3. What Tools Did I Use?
I decided to use Canva to help assist me with the creative workflow and design process.  Although this creative process was very user friendly I did manage to hit some roadblocks in my design process due to the limitations of the Canva tools. Certain color shades or fonts were not available, as well as some interactive features. Since I do not pay for a certain tier of the platform, it is very limiting on what features they offer.  Chat GPT and Claude were very helpful on assisting me with finding some alternate tools to include in my website to fulfill the feature request I was looking for which turned out to be some solid workarounds.
